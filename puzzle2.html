I AM ALONE

“Not in the sense of being physically isolated, but in the way it feels like no one truly sees what I’m going through. The weight of everything sits squarely on my shoulders, and there’s no one to share it with. Even when I’m surrounded by people, the silence inside me feels deafening. It’s like I’m screaming for help, but no one can hear me. Maybe they don’t notice, or maybe I’ve gotten too good at hiding it. Either way, it’s just me, facing this all by myself.”
