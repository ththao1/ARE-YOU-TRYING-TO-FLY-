JUDGING

“I feel like I’m constantly being judged, like every move I make is being watched and criticized. Even when people don’t say anything, their looks, their silence—it all feels heavy, like it’s meant to remind me I’m not enough. The words they do say stick with me, replaying in my head over and over, making me doubt myself even more. I start to believe their judgment, as if their opinions define who I am. It’s suffocating, like no matter what I do, I’ll never measure up. And the worst part is, I can’t seem to turn off that voice in my own head that keeps agreeing with them.”
